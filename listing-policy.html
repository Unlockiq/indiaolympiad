Listing policy

The rules we can be held to.

Inclusion, ordering, date labelling, and how the publisher's own conflict of interest is handled — in writing.

Every directory has rules. Ours are published, so you can hold us to them.

1 · Inclusion

We list established school olympiads and academic competitions open to students in India, on uniform criteria: organiser, classes, fee, format and window. Listing is free. No organiser can pay to be listed, and none can pay to be removed from comparison.

2 · Order and prominence

Calendar order follows the season. Directory order groups by subject. No position on any page is sold — to anyone, including the publisher.

3 · Date labelling

"Confirmed" appears only when the organiser has published the date. "Expected" marks our estimate from past seasons and is always labelled. When an organiser announces a change, we update on notice — corrections to dates are prioritised above all other site work.

4 · The publisher's conflict, handled in the open

This site is published by Unlock IQ Institute Pvt. Ltd., which organises four programmes listed here. We handle that conflict by disclosure and symmetry: the publisher's programmes appear in the same card format, same table rows, same criteria — and this page plus the site footer state the relationship plainly. If you believe any listing breaches this symmetry, use the contact page; such reports go to the top of the queue.

5 · What we don't do

  • No paid rankings, stars, or "editor's choice" badges — for anyone.
  • No prep-course sales on this site.
  • No data collection from children, anywhere on the site.

Effective July 2026. Changes to this policy are posted here with the date of change.
