The directory · 2026–27

41 olympiads. One uniform card each.

From the HBCSE national olympiad pathway to school-route exams — filtered by subject, honestly labelled, sources named.

41 olympiads and talent exams, identical criteria for each: organiser, classes, fee, format, window — with the publisher's own four listed under the same rules as everyone else. Cards are grouped by subject and alphabetised — order is never a ranking. 15 carry organiser-confirmed 2026-27 dates; the rest are marked expected until announced.
