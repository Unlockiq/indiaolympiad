SOF IHO — International Hindi Olympiad

Science Olympiad Foundation · Classes 3–10 · 2026–27 window: Oct 2026 – Jan 2027 (school-chosen date)

Hindi language and literature for Classes 3–10, single level.

One of the few national platforms rewarding Hindi proficiency competitively — worth noting for Hindi-medium schools.

Who is it for?

Students of Classes 3–10. Subject focus: GK / Multi / Other. As with any olympiad, the best candidates are children who voluntarily enjoy the subject — an olympiad should stretch curiosity, not manufacture pressure.

Registration & source of record

Official information and registration: sofworld.org. Always verify the current date, fee and eligibility on the organiser's own site before paying — organisers occasionally shift windows, and this page is a directory entry, not the organiser.
