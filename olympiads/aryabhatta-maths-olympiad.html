Aryabhatta Maths Olympiad (AMO)

Unlock IQ Institute · Classes 1–11 · 2026–27 window: L1: 24–25 Apr 2027 · L2: Jul 2027

Two-stage maths olympiad with national ranks, medals and study material. 48-hour flexible window at Level-1.

AMO runs on grade-level syllabi taught at olympiad difficulty — harder multi-step problems rather than next-year content. Every registrant gets mock exams; both levels are webcam-proctored. Study material follows a self-teachable, NCERT-inclined design with Indian mathematical heritage woven in.

Who is it for?

Students of Classes 1–11. Subject focus: Maths. As with any olympiad, the best candidates are children who voluntarily enjoy the subject — an olympiad should stretch curiosity, not manufacture pressure.

Registration & source of record

Official information and registration: aryabhattamathsolympiad.com. Always verify the current date, fee and eligibility on the organiser's own site before paying — organisers occasionally shift windows, and this page is a directory entry, not the organiser.
