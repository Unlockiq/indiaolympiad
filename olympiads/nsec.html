Chemistry · Expected

NSEC — National Standard Examination in Chemistry

IAPT / ACT · Classes 11–12 · 2026–27 window: Nov 2026 · reg opens 1 Aug 2026

First stage of the Chemistry Olympiad pathway toward INChO and the International Chemistry Olympiad.

Senior-secondary chemistry tested at depth — organic mechanisms, physical chemistry calculations and inorganic reasoning beyond routine board patterns. Qualifiers proceed to INChO.

Who is it for?

Students of Classes 11–12. Subject focus: Chemistry. As with any olympiad, the best candidates are children who voluntarily enjoy the subject — an olympiad should stretch curiosity, not manufacture pressure.

Registration & source of record

Official information and registration: iapt.org.in. Always verify the current date, fee and eligibility on the organiser's own site before paying — organisers occasionally shift windows, and this page is a directory entry, not the organiser.
