Olympiads for Class 12

25 exams open to Class 12 this season — 7 with organiser-confirmed dates. Honest guidance first, list second.

At Classes 11–12 the field splits: the HBCSE national olympiads (NSEP/NSEC/NSEB/NSEA → INOs) are the prestigious, JEE/NEET-compatible road; broad school-route olympiads still work for profile-building. Choose by ambition, and mind the board calendar.
