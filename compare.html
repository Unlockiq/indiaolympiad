How to read this table

Fee is per student; school-route fees sometimes differ from direct registration, and international formats cost more. Format matters practically: in-school exams need your school registered with the organiser; online proctored exams run from home; centre-based exams mean travel. The HBCSE pathway exams (IOQM, NSEs) are the serious end — cheap to enter, brutal to top, and the only road to representing India internationally.

Start from your child, not the exam: subject of highest curiosity first, workable format second, fee last. The choosing guide walks it in five minutes.
