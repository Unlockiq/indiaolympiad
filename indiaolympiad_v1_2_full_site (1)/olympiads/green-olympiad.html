GREEN Olympiad

TERI · Classes 4–12 · 2026–27 window: Sep – Nov 2026 (expected)

TERI's environment olympiad — sustainability, climate and ecology for Classes 4–12.

A meaningful pick for eco-minded students; certificates carry TERI's institutional weight. Often paired by schools with environment-week activities.

Who is it for?

Students of Classes 4–12. Subject focus: Science. As with any olympiad, the best candidates are children who voluntarily enjoy the subject — an olympiad should stretch curiosity, not manufacture pressure.

Registration & source of record

Official information and registration: terigreenolympiad.com. Always verify the current date, fee and eligibility on the organiser's own site before paying — organisers occasionally shift windows, and this page is a directory entry, not the organiser.
